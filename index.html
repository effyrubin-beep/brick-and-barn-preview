Common Questions

Honest answers.

Does filling in the questionnaire commit me to anything?

+

No. Submitting the questionnaire creates no obligation whatsoever. You can stop the process at any point, for any reason, at no cost.

How is this different from selling with an agent?

+

No listings, no showings, no commissions, no waiting. We buy directly — speed, privacy, no agent fees. The trade-off is you're selling to one buyer rather than the open market. We're transparent about that.

My property needs significant repairs. Is that a problem?

+

Not at all. We buy properties in all conditions. Condition factors into our offer structure — it doesn't disqualify your property. No money needed on fixes before we meet.

Can I have an attorney review the agreement?

+

Absolutely, and we encourage it. Our closing firm — Harrell, Martin & Peace — handles all title work and can assist sellers with questions.

What if I have tenants in the property?

+

We handle tenant-occupied properties regularly. Tenant situations are addressed as part of the purchase structure — not after the fact. We are familiar with SC landlord-tenant law.

How quickly can you close?

+

Our average close is 14 days after agreement. We can also slow down if you need more time — the timeline is yours to set.

What does "creative structure" mean?

+

Sale options beyond a simple cash offer — like seller financing or assuming your existing mortgage. We explain everything clearly and in plain language.

Is my information kept private?

+

Yes. Information shared through our questionnaire is never shared with third parties, listed publicly, or used for marketing. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
